About & Governance

Our commitment to excellence, transparency, and sustainable development drives everything we do at Trans Guatemala Pipeline.

Corporate Profile

Trans Guatemala Pipeline (TGP) is a pioneering energy infrastructure company dedicated to transforming global energy logistics through innovative pipeline solutions. Our flagship project represents one of the most significant energy infrastructure developments in Central America.

With a commitment to excellence, environmental stewardship, and community engagement, TGP is positioned to become a cornerstone of inter-oceanic energy transportation, connecting Atlantic and Pacific markets with unprecedented efficiency and reliability.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

To revolutionize global energy logistics by providing safe, efficient, and sustainable pipeline transportation solutions that connect markets, create value, and support economic development while maintaining the highest standards of environmental and social responsibility.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Our approach to community engagement is built on principles of respect, transparency, and mutual benefit. We work closely with local communities, government authorities, and civil society organizations to ensure that project development aligns with local needs and priorities.

Through ongoing consultation and feedback mechanisms, we maintain open dialogue with all stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle, from planning and construction through long-term operations.
